# Hot Pink Sedan — Top-Down Pixel Art Game Sprite

## Style
Crisp hard-edged PIXEL ART matching the existing sedan sprites in this set (same "big pixel" scale, outline weight, flat-shading). NOT photorealistic, NOT anti-aliased/blurry.

## Product Description
A compact top-down sedan sprite, one of the passenger cars in a 12-car color set. Base color hot pink (#EC4899) with 2-3 flat shading blocks (lighter highlight along centerline, darker magenta shadow along side edges). Windshield/rear window as flat near-black (#1A1A1F) rectangles. Thin black outline around the whole silhouette. Same scale and proportions as the other sedan sprites in this set.

## Wheel Position — CRITICAL, must be pixel-precise
Spans exactly 2 cells lengthwise. Four wheels (small black squares):
- Front-left/front-right: centered at exactly 75% of sprite width
- Rear-left/rear-right: centered at exactly 25% of sprite width
- Vertically centered (50% of sprite height)

## Background
Solid flat pure magenta chroma-key background, hex #FF00FF, uniform, hard clean edge — for chroma-key alpha extraction.

## Angles & Composition — near-zero margin
Top-down, nose pointing right. Frame width = EXACTLY two grid-cell-lengths. Front/rear tips within ~2% of left/right edges. Roughly 2:1 landscape aspect.
